## Character Identity

- **Playstyle**: High HP, aggressive. Excels at Strength scaling, self-damage synergies, and Exhaust mechanics.
- **Strengths**: Highest base HP (80), strong damage output, powerful scaling through Strength and Demon Form.
- **Weaknesses**: Limited card draw, weaker defensive options compared to other characters.
- **Key synergies**: Strength stacking, Exhaust triggers, self-healing, Vulnerable application.
